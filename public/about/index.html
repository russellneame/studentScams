About

StudentScams is a platform that was created in order to help raise awareness about different scams that students face. This platform gives users the ability to read through other peoples posts, as well as share their own experiences or warnings, which in turn will aim to increase students knowledge of the scams and result in reducing students susceptibility to scams.
